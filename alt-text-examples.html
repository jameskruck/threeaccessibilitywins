📝 ALT TEXT WORKSHOP

Interactive Alt Text Examples

These examples show real alt text from workplace documents, expert feedback on common problems, and improved versions. Click "Show Improved Version" to reveal the final result after expert review.

Original Alt Text: "A supervisor with a hazard document in his hand, planning safe work. Two construction members are in the back picking up lumber building a house"

🔍 Expert Feedback

Please cut "planning safe work". What is the visual clue that helps the learner draw that conclusion in their mind: does the document have plans on it? In the same way, "building a house" should either be cut or rephrased. Is there a half-built house in the background that the learner should know about?

Improved Alt Text: "A supervisor holds a blue folder with a hazard symbol on it. In the background, two workers carry lumber from the frame of a house."
Original Alt Text: "A man is sitting down, facing the left in deep thought. He has thinking bubbles above him, on the right with a question mark in it."

🔍 Expert Feedback

I'd cut "in deep thought". I'm curious about the question mark: is it inside one of the bubbles? Or is there a question mark in every bubble?

Improved Alt Text: "A man is sitting down, facing the left. He has a thought bubble above him, on the right with a question mark in it."
Original Alt Text: "Four people stand in a circle facing each other, appearing to have a conversation. Starting on the left and moving clockwise, there is a person with long dark hair holding a blue folder, a person with short dark hair whose arms are folded with one hand on their chin, a person with short red hair and an artificial leg holding a folder with one arm outstretched towards the centre of the circle, and a person with short dark hair with both arms outstretched."

🔍 Expert Feedback

Change to "[number of] people putting a puzzle together." What are one or two other visual details that jump out at the sighted learner? Are the people in this picture the same people from any of the earlier pictures?

Improved Alt Text: "Cartoon graphic of 3 people putting a large puzzle together. One person is standing on a ladder to reach the top of a high spot to put a puzzle piece in its spot. The other two people are standing next to the puzzle holding pieces."
Original Alt Text: (No alt text provided)

🔍 Expert Feedback

I'd rephrase as: "A person in a hard hat holds a clipboard with jagged lines coming out of it, and stands facing a slumped person looking at the ground."

Improved Alt Text: "A person in a hard hat holds a clipboard with jagged lines coming out of it, and stands facing a slumped person looking at the ground."
Original Alt Text: "A professionally dressed man sits at a table."

🔍 Expert Feedback

What are the details that make the man "look[...] professional"?

Improved Alt Text: "A person with neat, short hair wearing a dark suit and tie sits at a table."

Key Takeaways for Alt Text

  • Be specific about visual details rather than making interpretations
  • Avoid assumptions about what people are thinking or feeling
  • Include relevant context that sighted users would notice immediately
  • Keep it concise while including essential information
  • Test with real users when possible to validate your descriptions

Workshop Activity

Interactive Exercise: Before revealing the improved versions, have participants work in pairs to:

  1. Read the original alt text and expert feedback
  2. Write their own improved version
  3. Compare their version with the expert-reviewed final version
  4. Discuss what they learned about describing images objectively
